Last 10 Days Review

And when I struggle, I know that I’ll be able to come ahead

Sometimes (I should say, almost always), is not easy to go on and keep the things the planned way.

There are struggles in the way, time issues, some incidents, things that come up and we never thought about before. All this things make us feel powerless and inhibit the progress. Although many of this things are or can be taken as positive.

Last week was one of those weeks that let me completely tired, frustrated by one side and incredibly happy by another.

The side that lead me to frustration was that for 3 days, one of the music courses was not available. So I went on with the Theory lessons, but not with the practical course. I finished all lessons, but I have neither done the sumup yet nor the exercises for the weekly lessons.

Also, as almost every night was really busy, for I have a major deadline at work soon, I’m passing job interviews and also keep on an ONG and talking about possibilities with 2 others, one in education and the other on social development. This tired me, and this week and the following will be more or less the same.

This affected my music studies, and the development of the web apps I’m working on. But on the plus side, I made a new contact and entered in nice discussions about online software for education and practice. I hope this leads to something interesting.

As Sunday night I saw the class website was back online, I downloaded all the videos, and I finished watching last 3 weeks of the course. So I hurried up.

I did practice every day, and I didn’t manage to learn well the second exercise on the Czerny Op.599.I’ll keep on working on it, it’ll take time, patience and the best part is: I will succeed.
